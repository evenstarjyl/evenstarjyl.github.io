"Dear Friends"

Dr. James A. Craig Memorial Fund

Dr. Craig was a veterinary surgeon. He dedicated his life to animals and specialized in orthopedic and reconstructive surgery. He also had a special interest in Oncology. As one of Dr Jyl Rubin's (VARK Founder and President) mentors, he encouraged her to continue pursuing her life dream of becoming a veterinarian.

"Dear Companions"

Turbo Fund

The Turbo Fund was created and is dedicated to providing medical care for felines in need. Funds are reviewed and approved by the V.A.R.K. Board of Directors on a case-by-case basis. The Turbo Fund was created by Barbara Zak and Anthony Santos to pay respect to a beloved family member lost, their handsome boy, TURBO. If you would like to make a dedicated donation to the Turbo Fund, you may do so either through PayPal or my mail.

Turtle Fund

The TURTLE FUND was created to raise funds to build our special shelled friends their own little pond. There are many ways you can help us achieve our goal: (1) Make a dedicated donation to the "Turtle Fund" through either PayPal or by mail. (2) Do you own a backhoe, do you have a little extra time on your hands, do you like to play in the dirt? If you answered YES to these questions, you can help. All you need to do is give V.A.R.K. a call at (916) 989-0733 or send an email to vark@varkinfo.org and let us know you would like to help!
